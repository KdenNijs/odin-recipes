3 Ingredient Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Delicious, simple and healthy. What are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • Banananananas
  • Rolled oats
  • Chocolate
  • Optional: cinnamon
  • Optional: sunflower seeds

Steps

  1. Mash bananas and rolled oats.
  2. Add the cinnamon and mix well.
  3. Chop the chocolate into nice chunks.
  4. Add chocolate and seeds.
  5. Form balls from the mixture and baaaake (~20min).
